Fares. There are two types of fares, variable and quoted.

  • Variable Fares. Variable fares consist of a base charge and incremental charges based on the duration and distance of your ride. We will use this information to provide a suggested price for you to start your bid. Please note that we use GPS data from your Driver’s phone to calculate the distance traveled on your ride. We cannot guarantee the availability or accuracy of GPS data. If we lose signal we will calculate time and distance using available data from your ride.

  • Bided Fares. Bidded fares are when riders use the Variable Fares to calculate a fare that best suits their price they are willing to pay. Riders send their bid to drivers who will either accept or Counter Offer with a bid of their own for the riders to either accept or decline.

  • Counter Offer. In some cases drivers may not accept the riders initial bid and counter offer for a different price at the time of your request. The Counter Offer can be accepted or declined by the rider. If during your ride you change your destination, make multiple stops, or attempt to abuse the Yibby Platform, we may cancel the fare quote and charge you a variable fare based on the time and distance of your ride. Yibby does not guarantee that the quoted fare price will be equal to a variable fare for the same ride.
